Doraemon Movies

Doraemon (ドラえもん) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Fujiko F. Fujio. First serialized in 1969, it has been adapted into a successful anime series and media franchise. The story follows a robotic cat named Doraemon, who travels back in time from the 22nd century to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi navigate life's challenges with the help of futuristic gadgets.
